About

Bute art Retreat is situated on the shores of a beautiful Kames Bay on the holiday isle of Bute. The Isle of Bute, the most accessible Western Scottish island lies within an easy reach of Glasgow International airport and only 1.30 min train and ferry journey from Glasgow. The full guide to things to do on Bute can be found here 10 Best Things to do in Isle of Bute

Immerse in relaxing atmosphere and enjoy breathtaking landscapes, walks and cycling on the isle of Bute. Mountain bikes or e-bikes can be rented from Bike Bute situated 100 m from Bute Art Retreat . Port Bannatyne Golf course is situated just 500 m away from Bute Art Retreat.

The guest who booked one of our packages will have a full day access to first floor 104 square meters studio room, accommodation in en-suite rooms situated in 17th century fishman's cottage as well as served healthy breakfast, lunch and dinner which include vegetarian and vegan options. The packages can adjusted to the guests' requirements.
